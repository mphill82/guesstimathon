• Guesstimathon

  • Time: 30:00
    Tries Left: 18
  • Raw score: 10
    Final score: 8192

Instructions:
Guess the positive answer to each question by submitting a positive lower and upper bound. The game ends when the time is up or you use all your tries. You can answer a question as many times as you like but you only get 18 tries total.

Scoring:
Get the lowest score by answering correctly with small intervals. A correct interval (a,b) will add b/a (rounded down) to the raw score so a small interval is best. Each incorrect or unanswered question doubles your raw score to give your final score. Your raw score starts at 10. Your final score starts at 8192 because 10 doubled 13 times for the unanswered questions is 8192.

Examples:
Abraham guesses there are (40,55) states in the US, Brian guesses (33,89), and Charlie guesses (55,60). Abraham is correct with a relatively small interval and 55/40=1.375 so we round down and add only 1 his raw score. Brain is also correct with a larger interval and 89/33=2.69 so we round down and add 2 to his raw score. Neither Abraham's for Brian's final score will get doubled for this question. Charlie's final score gets doubled because his interval does not contain the correct answer.
