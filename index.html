• Guesstimathon

  • Time: 30:00
  • Tries: 18
  • Correct: 0
  • Score: 10

Instructions:
Guess the positive answer to each question by submitting a positive lower and upper bound. The game ends when the time is up or you use all your tries. You can answer a question as many times as you like but you only get 18 tries total.

Scoring:
Try to get a low score by answering correctly with small intervals. A correct interval (a,b) will add b/a (rounded down) to your score so a small interval is best. Each question left incorrect or unanswered will double your final score.

Examples:
Abraham guesses there are (49,55) states in the US, Brian guesses (33,89), and Charlie guesses (55,60). Abraham is correct with a relatively small interval and 55/49=1.122 so we round down and add only 1 his score. Brain is also correct with a larger interval and 89/33=2.69 so we round down and add 2 to his score. Neither Abraham's nor Brian's final score will get doubled for this question. Charlie's final score gets doubled because his interval does not contain the correct answer.
